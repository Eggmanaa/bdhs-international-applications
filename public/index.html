The Bishop Diego Difference

Care. Nurture. Support.

Most American high schools enroll international students. Very few invest in them the way we do. Our dedicated International Program Coordinator teaches, advises, and advocates for every international student across all four years.

C

Care

A dedicated International Program Coordinator is available daily for academic support across every subject. Your student is known by name, not by a number.

N

Nurture

Our International Buddy System pairs every new international student with a local peer, building friendships and cultural integration from day one. Mandarin-speaking staff provide additional support for Chinese families.

S

Support

Individualized college essay coaching, test preparation, and ongoing communication with families in their preferred language. Modified assignments and assessments when English language development calls for it.

How to Apply

Three Steps to Enrollment

Each step is straightforward. Mr. Eggman and the international team will guide you through the process personally.

1

Submit an Application

Complete the online application below. Plan on 45 to 60 minutes and have your documents ready to upload.

Go to Application

2

Submit Documents

Upload the following documents with your application:

  1. Current school year transcripts
  2. Academic transcripts from the prior two school years
  3. Photo of the student's passport
  4. Photo of the student's birth certificate
  5. Proof of financial responsibility — a recent bank statement or bank-certified letter for the person paying tuition. Do not submit tax documents.
  6. Two or three letters of recommendation from current teachers
  7. An email address where the I-20 form should be sent
3

Personal Admissions Interview

Mr. Eggman will reach out to schedule a personal admissions interview via Zoom. Once all steps are complete, you will be notified of our admission decision by email.

Once you pay the tuition deposit, we will issue your I-20.
