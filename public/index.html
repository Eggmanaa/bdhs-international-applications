The Bishop Diego Difference

Care. Nurture. Support.

Most American high schools enroll international students. Very few invest in them the way we do. A dedicated International Program Coordinator teaches, advises, and advocates for every international student across all four years.

Care

A dedicated International Program Coordinator is available daily for academic support across every subject. Your student is known by name, not by a number.

Nurture

With only 262 students, our international students are woven into the full Bishop Diego community from day one — known by name by faculty, friends with their domestic classmates, and integrated into clubs, athletics, and campus ministry.

Support

Individualized college essay coaching, test preparation, and ongoing communication with families in their preferred language. Modified assignments when English language development calls for it.

Academics

Rigor That Prepares Students for Elite Universities

Seventeen Advanced Placement courses. An 89% AP pass rate. Specialized College Board training for every AP teacher. Hands-on labs through the STEAM Society and SciTrek partnership with UC Santa Barbara. Bishop Diego students don't just check boxes, they build a foundation for university-level work.

World-Class Facilities

Real Tools for Real Skills

A professional-grade Bishop Diego TV broadcasting studio. A sound recording studio. Modern science labs. A one-to-one iPad program with digital textbooks. Our facilities aren't decoration, they're where students learn to make things the world pays attention to.

How to Apply

Three Steps to Enrollment

Each step is straightforward. Mr. Eggman and Ms. Diaz will guide you through the process personally.

1

Submit an Application

Complete the online application below. Plan on 45 to 60 minutes. Upload documents as you have them, you can come back to add more later using your unique upload link.

Go to Application

2

Submit Documents

Upload these with your application or add them later:

  1. Current school year transcripts
  2. Academic transcripts from the prior two school years
  3. Photo of the student's passport
  4. Photo of the student's birth certificate
  5. Proof of financial responsibility, a recent bank statement or bank-certified letter for the person paying tuition. Do not submit tax documents.
  6. Two or three letters of recommendation from current teachers
  7. An email address where the I-20 form should be sent
3

Personal Admissions Interview

Mr. Eggman will reach out to schedule a personal admissions interview via Zoom. Once all steps are complete, you will be notified of our admission decision by email.

Once you pay the tuition deposit, we will issue your I-20.

Already applied? If you've already submitted an application and need to add more documents, use the unique upload link that was shown after your application, or email them directly to Mr. Eggman at aeggman@bishopdiego.org.
